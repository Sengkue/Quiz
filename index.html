3. ຈົ່ງເລືອກຄໍາຕອບທີ່ຖືກຕ້ອງ, ຈະໄດ້ຫຍັງຈາກ SQL query ລຸ່ມນີ້: 
SELECT department, AVG(salary) AS average_salary FROM employees GROUP BY department;
  • A) ເງິນເດືອນທັງໝົດຂອງແຕ່ລະພະແນກ
  • B) ເງິນເດືອນສູງສຸດຂອງແຕ່ລະພະແນກ
  • C) ເງິນເດືອນຕໍາ່ສຸດທີ່ແຕ່ລະພະແນກ
  • D) ເງິນເດືອນສະເລ່ຍຂອງແຕ່ລະພະແນກ
4. ຈົ່ງສັງເກດເບິ່ງຕາຕະລາງ ທັງສອງຕາຕະລາງດັ່ງລຸ່ມນີ້ ແລ້ວເລືອກ SQL Query ເພື່ອ ຊອກຫາຈໍານວນການຂາຍທັງໝົດສໍາລັບພະນັກງານແຕ່ລະຄົນໃນພະແນກການຂາຍ: 
SELECT e.name, SUM(s.amount) AS total_sales FROM employees e JOIN sales s ON e.id = s.employee_id WHERE e.department = 'Sales' GROUP BY e.name;
  • A) SELECT e.name, SUM(s.amount) AS total_sales FROM employees e JOIN sales s ON e.id = s.employee_id GROUP BY e.name;
  • B) SELECT e.name, SUM(s.amount) AS total_sales FROM employees e LEFT JOIN sales s ON e.id = s.employee_id WHERE e.department = 'Sales' GROUP BY e.name;
  • C) SELECT e.name, SUM(s.amount) AS total_sales FROM employees e INNER JOIN sales s ON e.id = s.employee_id GROUP BY e.name;
  • D) SELECT e.name, SUM(s.amount) AS total_sales FROM employees e JOIN sales s ON e.id = s.employee_id WHERE e.department = 'Sales' GROUP BY e.id;
